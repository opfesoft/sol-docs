## Description of the fields

### guid

The GUID of the character. See [characters.guid](characters.md#guid)

### slot

Arena slot index:

0 - 2v2

1 - 3v3

2 - 5v5

### matchMakerRating

Player's matchmaker rating

### maxMMR

Player's maximum matchmaker rating
